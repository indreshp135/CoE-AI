Vision

The TATA Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning will help to build a nationally recognized AI ecosystem centered on NIT Trichy.

Mission

Our vision is to establish a prestigious, cutting-edge facility capable of developing innovative AI-enabled solutions. It will help with the development of AI software and hardware, mentoring and professional skill acquisition, and job creation. Furthermore, it will support breakthroughs in AI ethics and socially conscious policies. Our goal is to contribute to the development of technologies that boost and enhance the human experience.

Director's Message



Dr. G. Aghila
Director, NIT-T

The TATA Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning facilitate targeted and predominantly AI based capacity building programmes in areas that benefit the sector at large. The Centre serve as a focal point for sharing of best practices in the areas that benefit the students, institutes, industry and other related organization. The TATA Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning build a creative research environment and strengthens the exchange of ideas across the student community, faculty members and domain experts, also provide information and referral service for academic and research work on AI related areas.
