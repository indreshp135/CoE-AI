Internship

UG / PG Student Internship and UG/PG fresh graduate Internship opportunity is provided in the Centre. The selected candidate interns will be working in core field and application development. The intern should work on the problem assigned by the Faculty Mentor within the short period of time (3 months or 12 months based on category).


Period of Internship:
Short term internship (3 months):
Preferably during vacation or candidate can carry out their final year UG project work duration last semester.
Long term internship (1 year):
Candidate can carry out their PG project work or those who complete UG / PG (fresher’s) can be given an opportunity.
